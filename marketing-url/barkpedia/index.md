# Identify Any Dog Breed — Instantly, Privately, and Free

See a dog you love but can't name the breed? Barkpedia gives you the answer in seconds. Just point your camera, snap a photo, and get an accurate breed match — no internet required, no account to create, and no cost. Ever.

## Why Dog Lovers Choose Barkpedia

- **Satisfy your curiosity on the spot** — At the park, the shelter, or a friend's house, get a breed ID in seconds and learn everything about the dog in front of you.
- **Use it anywhere — no Wi-Fi needed** — Breed identification runs entirely on your iPhone. Works on hikes, road trips, or anywhere cell service doesn't reach.
- **100% free with zero catches** — No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. Download it once and use it forever.
- **Your photos never leave your device** — No cloud uploads, no accounts, no tracking. Complete privacy by design.

## More to Discover

- **Breed Profiles** — Dive into temperament, size, energy level, history, and photos for every breed you identify.
- **Favorites** — Save your scan results and revisit them anytime, all stored locally.
- **Breed Quiz** — Think you know your Shiba from your Akita? Put your knowledge to the test.
- **Dog Facts** — Surprise yourself with fun trivia about man's best friend.

## How It Works

1. **Open the app** — no sign-up, ready in seconds
2. **Snap a photo** of any dog, or choose one from your library
3. **Get your result** — breed name, confidence score, and a detailed breed profile

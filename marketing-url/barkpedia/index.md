# See a dog you love? Know the breed in seconds.

Barkpedia identifies dog breeds instantly using on-device machine learning. Just point your camera or pick a photo from your library and get an accurate match — no internet required, no account to create, and no cost. Ever.

## Why dog lovers choose Barkpedia

- **Satisfy your curiosity on the spot** — At the park, the shelter, or a friend's house, identify a breed in seconds and learn everything about the dog in front of you.
- **Works anywhere — no Wi-Fi needed** — Breed identification runs entirely on your device. Works on hikes, road trips, or anywhere cell service doesn't reach.
- **100% free with zero catches** — No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. Download it once and use it forever.
- **Your photos never leave your device** — No cloud uploads, no accounts, no tracking. Complete privacy by design.

## More to discover

- **147 Breed Profiles** — Temperament, size, energy level, origin, history, and photos for every breed you identify.
- **Favorites** — Save your scan results and revisit them anytime, all stored locally.
- **Breed Quiz** — 10-question photo rounds. Think you know your Shiba from your Akita? Put your knowledge to the test and earn reward titles.
- **Dog Trivia** — True-or-false rounds mixing real dog facts with plausible myths. How well do you really know dogs?

## How it works

1. **Open the app** — no sign-up, ready in seconds
2. **Snap a photo** of any dog, or choose one from your library
3. **Get your result** — breed name, confidence score, and a detailed breed profile

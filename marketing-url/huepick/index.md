# Turn everyday inspiration into colors that last.

Huepick extracts dominant colors from any photo and generates harmonious palettes using color theory — all processed on your device. No account required, and completely free.

## Why Huepick?

- **Works Offline** — No Wi-Fi? No problem. Core features run locally on your device with no internet required.
- **100% Free** — No in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no ads.
- **Private by Design** — Your photos never leave your device. No accounts, no analytics.

## Features

- **Color Extraction** — Point your camera at anything or pick a photo from your library. Huepick finds up to 12 dominant colors in seconds.
- **Smart Palettes** — Generate harmonious color palettes based on classical color theory: complementary, analogous, triadic, split complementary, tetradic, and monochromatic.
- **Real-World Scenarios** — Get tailored palettes for Home Decor, Car Interior, Fashion, Makeup, and UI & Graphic Design — each with context-appropriate color roles.
- **Save & Edit** — Save palettes to your gallery, edit individual colors, or add custom colors using the built-in color picker.
- **Copy Hex Codes** — Tap any color to copy its hex code instantly.
- **Photo Context** — Palettes remember where and when the photo was taken, so you can revisit your inspiration.

## How It Works

1. **Take or pick a photo** — no account needed
2. **Extract colors** — Huepick identifies up to 12 dominant colors
3. **Generate palettes** — choose a harmony type and use case to get a tailored palette
4. **Save & copy** — keep palettes in your gallery and copy hex codes

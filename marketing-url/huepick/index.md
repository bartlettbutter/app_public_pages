# Turn everyday inspiration into perfect color palettes.

Huepick extracts dominant colors from any photo and generates harmonious palettes using color theory — all processed on your device. No account required, completely free.

## Why creatives choose Huepick

- **Capture colors from the real world** — Point your camera at anything inspiring and extract up to 12 dominant colors in seconds. Take the guesswork out of color matching.
- **Palettes grounded in color theory** — Generate harmonious combinations using six classical harmony types: complementary, analogous, triadic, split complementary, tetradic, and monochromatic.
- **Tailored to your project** — Get context-aware palettes for Home Decor, Fashion, Makeup, Car Interior, and UI & Graphic Design — each with meaningful color roles.
- **Works offline, stays private** — All processing happens on your device. No Wi-Fi needed, no accounts, no analytics, no ads.

## Features

- **Smart Color Extraction** — Identifies up to 12 dominant colors from any photo.
- **6 Harmony Types** — Classical color theory applied automatically to your extracted colors.
- **5 Real-World Scenarios** — Palettes with context-appropriate roles (wall/floor/accent, hair/skin/top/bottom, lip/eye/blush/contour, primary/secondary/background, and more).
- **Save & Edit** — Build a gallery of palettes, edit individual colors, or create custom palettes from scratch.
- **Copy Hex Codes** — Tap any swatch to copy its hex code instantly.
- **Photo Context** — Palettes remember where and when the photo was taken, so you can revisit your inspiration.

## How it works

1. **Take or pick a photo** — no account needed
2. **Extract colors** — Huepick identifies up to 12 dominant colors
3. **Generate palettes** — choose a harmony type and use case
4. **Save & copy** — keep palettes in your gallery and copy hex codes

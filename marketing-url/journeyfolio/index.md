# Your entire trip, organized in one place.

Journeyfolio takes the chaos out of travel planning. Build detailed itineraries, check live weather forecasts powered by [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html), and track your flights in real time — all stored on your device and accessible offline.

## Plan Your Way

- **Day-by-Day Itineraries** — Organize destinations, activities, notes, and timing for each day of your trip.
- **Multi-City Trips** — Plan trips across multiple cities. Each destination gets its own itinerary, weather forecast, and date range.
- **Works Offline** — All your trip data lives on your device. Access your itinerary anytime, anywhere — no internet needed.

## Stay Informed

- **Live Flight Tracking** — Add a flight number and get real-time status updates powered by FlightAware, including delay and cancellation alerts.
- **Weather at a Glance** — See current conditions and 5-day forecasts for every destination, powered by [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html).

## Keep Everything Together

Track every detail of your trip in one place:

- **Flights** — Status, departure/arrival times, airline info, and seat numbers.
- **Hotels** — Names, confirmation numbers, check-in/check-out dates, addresses, and phone numbers.
- **Transport** — Car rentals, trains, buses, ferries, taxis, shuttles, and more.
- **Restaurants** — Names, addresses, and phone numbers.
- **Places & Tickets** — Attractions, museums, shows, tours, parks, and shopping.
- **Insurance** — Travel insurance details and notes.

## Helpful Extras

- **Location Search** — Find nearby hotels, restaurants, and points of interest and add them to your itinerary.
- **Public Holidays** — See local holidays for your destinations so you can plan around closures and events.
- **Pin Trips** — Keep your most important trips at the top of the list for quick access.

## Simple by Design

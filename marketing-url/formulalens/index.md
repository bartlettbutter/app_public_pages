# Know what's really in your skincare.

You shouldn't need a chemistry degree to understand your products. Snap a photo of any ingredient list and FormulaLens tells you what every ingredient does, why it matters for your product type, and how two products compare — all in seconds, entirely on your device.

## What FormulaLens does for you

- **See through the label** — photograph any ingredient list and get a clear, color-coded breakdown showing what each ingredient actually does for your skin. No searching, no typing, no guesswork.
- **Make smarter choices** — FormulaLens flags key ingredients that serve your product's purpose and highlights anything unusual for that category, so you can shop with confidence.
- **Compare before you buy** — scan multiple products and see what they share, what's unique, and how their formulas differ side by side.
- **Keep your data yours** — everything happens on your device. No uploads, no accounts, no tracking. Your photos and results never leave your phone.

## How it works

1. **Choose your product type** — select from 13 categories like Moisturizer, Sunscreen, Serum, or Shampoo
2. **Snap or select a photo** — photograph the ingredient list or pick an image from your library
3. **Read your results** — every ingredient is color-coded and labeled with its function, relevance, and a plain-language explanation
4. **Go deeper** — tap any ingredient for detailed info and links to trusted cosmetic databases

## Go beyond a single scan

- **Compare products** — see shared vs. unique ingredients across your routine with a visual breakdown chart
- **Build your library** — save scans with custom labels and revisit full analyses anytime
- **Research further** — one-tap links to INCIDecoder, COSMILE Europe, and EWG Skin Deep for every ingredient

## Built for the ingredient-conscious

**Skincare enthusiasts** building a routine and wanting to understand what each product actually delivers. **Ingredient-sensitive shoppers** checking for fragrances, exfoliants, or preservatives before buying. **K-beauty and J-beauty fans** decoding long INCI lists on imported products. **Parents** vetting what goes on their family's skin. **Anyone** who's ever flipped a bottle over and thought, "What is all this?"

# Your daily edge in ETF investing.

Stop scrolling through endless tickers. ETFWise delivers curated daily ETF recommendations across seven categories, backed by live market data, real-time sentiment, and private AI analysis that never leaves your device.

## Why investors choose ETFWise

- **Fresh picks every morning** across Major Index, Sector, Bond, International, Commodity, Thematic, and Dividend categories, refreshed daily so you always know where to look.
- **See what moves the market** with news sentiment scores, prediction market probabilities, and analyst consensus giving you the full picture before you act.
- **AI analysis that stays private** using an on-device model that generates bull and risk cases from real metrics. No cloud uploads. No data leaves your device.
- **Live data, zero cost** with real-time prices refreshing every 5 minutes, 200+ curated ETFs, and 400+ searchable tickers. No subscriptions. No ads. Ever.

## Deep analysis at your fingertips

Tap any ETF for comprehensive insight:

- **Price, stats, and fund overview** including current price, expense ratio, AUM, and tracking index
- **Top holdings and sector weights** so you know exactly what you own
- **Performance history** with trailing returns, annual returns, best/worst years, and Morningstar rating
- **News sentiment** showing a 7-day sentiment score with the latest headlines
- **AI-generated bull and risk cases** with context-rich analysis grounded in real data
- **Prediction markets** with crowd-sourced probabilities relevant to each ETF
- **Analyst consensus** showing Wall Street buy/hold/sell ratings at a glance

## Get started in seconds

1. **Download** ETFWise free from the App Store
2. **Add a free API key** from Finnhub for live data, or skip and explore with built-in demo data
3. **Browse today's picks** across seven categories
4. **Tap any ETF** for deep analysis, sentiment, and AI-powered insights

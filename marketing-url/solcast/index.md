# Weather that tells you what to do, not just what it is.

Solcast turns raw forecasts into personalized, actionable guidance — what to wear, what to watch out for, and how to plan your day. Free, no ads, no subscriptions.

## Why people switch to Solcast

- **Skip the guesswork** — Get plain-language advice instead of decoding numbers. Solcast tells you when to grab sunscreen, pack an umbrella, or keep the kids in extra layers.
- **Personalized to your life** — 11 settings let you dial in your temperature sensitivity, dressing style, allergy triggers, household members, and more. Every recommendation adapts instantly.
- **Private by design** — All recommendations are computed on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. Your location is used only to fetch weather and is never stored on any server.
- **Completely free** — No in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no ads. Ever.

## What you get

- **16 Lifestyle Indices** — UV & SPF, flu risk, allergy, air quality, comfort, hydration, and more — translated into clear action items.
- **Outfit Recommendations** — Daily clothing picks matched to your dressing style and the day's conditions.
- **Today's Briefing** — A glanceable card with everything you need to know before you step outside.
- **7-Day Forecast** — Temperature, wind, UV, precipitation, air quality, and pollen at a glance.
- **Multi-Location** — Save up to 10 locations and switch between them instantly.

## Built for real life

- **Parents** — Reminders about kids' sunscreen, rain boots, and extra layers
- **Pet owners** — Know when it's safe for walks, paw protection alerts, and storm warnings
- **Allergy sufferers** — Pollen and air quality alerts calibrated to your sensitivity
- **Migraine-prone** — Pressure and altitude-based headache warnings
- **Outdoor exercisers** — Optimal workout timing based on conditions
- **Anyone who hates overthinking what to wear**

## Get started in seconds

1. **Launch** — no account needed, no sign-up
2. **Allow location** (or search for a city manually)
3. **See your personalized dashboard** — weather, briefing, outfit, and indices load automatically
4. **Fine-tune in Settings** — changes apply instantly

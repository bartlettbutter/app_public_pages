# Know how the weather affects your day — before you step outside.

Solcast combines real-time weather data with personalized lifestyle guidance so you always know what to wear, what to watch out for, and how to plan your day. Free, no ads, no subscriptions.

## The Problem

Standard weather apps show you numbers — temperature, humidity, UV index — but leave you to figure out what they mean for your day. Should you bring an umbrella? Do you need sunscreen? Is it safe to exercise outside? What should your kids wear?

## The Solution

Solcast translates raw weather data into practical, personalized advice. It computes 16 lifestyle indices, generates outfit recommendations, and delivers a daily briefing — all tailored to your sensitivity, style, and household.

## Who Is Solcast For?

Anyone who wants weather presented as actionable guidance rather than raw data. Especially useful for:

- **Parents** — reminders about kids' sunscreen, rain boots, and extra layers
- **Pet owners** — know when it's safe for walks, paw protection, and storm warnings
- **Allergy sufferers** — pollen and air quality alerts at your sensitivity level
- **Migraine-sensitive individuals** — pressure and altitude-based headache warnings
- **Outdoor exercisers** — optimal workout timing based on conditions
- **Anyone who hates overthinking what to wear**

## Features

- **16 Lifestyle Indices** — From UV and SPF to flu risk, allergy, air quality, comfort, hydration, and more — actionable answers, not just numbers.
- **Outfit Recommendations** — Daily clothing picks personalized to your dressing style and weather conditions.
- **Today's Briefing** — A glanceable card with plain-language action items for your day.
- **7-Day Forecast** — Comprehensive weather data including temperature, wind, UV, precipitation, air quality, and pollen.
- **10 Personalization Settings** — Temperature sensitivity, dressing style, sun/allergy/migraine sensitivity, kids, pets, cars — all recalculated instantly.
- **Multi-Location** — Save multiple locations and switch between them instantly.

## How It Works

1. **Launch** — no account needed, no sign-up
2. **Allow location** (or search for a city manually)
3. **Get your personalized dashboard** — weather, briefing, outfit, and indices load automatically
4. **Customize in Settings** — fine-tune to your preferences; changes apply instantly

## Your Privacy, Protected

All recommendations are computed on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. Your location is used only to fetch weather data and is never stored on any server.

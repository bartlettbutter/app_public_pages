# How can we help?

We want Huepick to work beautifully for you. If something isn't quite right or you're wondering how a feature works, you'll likely find your answer below. If not, we're just an email away.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I extract colors from a photo?**
Open the Analyze tab, take a photo or choose one from your library, and Huepick will automatically identify up to 12 dominant colors. No setup required.

**Do I need to create an account?**
No. Huepick has no accounts, no sign-up, and no login. Just open the app and start using it.

**Is an internet connection required?**
Almost everything works offline — color extraction, palettes, creative tools, and saving. The only optional network feature is converting photo location coordinates into a place name (like "Seattle, WA") on saved palettes.

**Is Huepick really free?**
Yes. No in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no ads. Every feature is available from the moment you download.

### Using the App

**What are the three tabs?**
- **Analyze** — take or pick a photo, extract colors, generate palettes, view detailed color analysis, and get personalized creative tool suggestions
- **Playground** — transform your photo with five creative tools (Isolate, Duotone, Pixelize, Blur, Glitch)
- **Gallery** — browse, re-edit, download, or share your saved creations

**How do I generate a palette?**
After extracting colors, choose one of six harmony types (like Complementary or Analogous) and one of five real-world scenarios (like Home Decor or UI Design). Huepick builds a palette with labeled color roles tailored to your project.

**How do I copy a hex code?**
Tap any color swatch. A brief confirmation appears to let you know the hex code is on your clipboard.

**What are the creative tools in Playground?**
- **Isolate** — keep selected colors vibrant while the rest becomes grayscale (or vice versa)
- **Duotone** — remap your image to two tones for a bold, editorial look
- **Pixelize** — transform into geometric pixel art with adjustable block size
- **Blur** — add soft, dreamy focus with adjustable intensity
- **Glitch** — apply RGB channel separation and digital distortion

**How do I save a creation?**
When using any tool in the Playground, you'll see buttons to save to your Gallery, download to your Photos library, or share directly. You can also reset and try a different look.

**Can I re-edit something I saved?**
Yes. In the Gallery, long-press any creation and choose "Edit in Playground" to reopen it with the creative tools.

**How do I delete a creation?**
Long-press the creation in the Gallery and choose "Delete." You'll be asked to confirm.

### Privacy & Data

**Where is my data stored?**
Everything — saved palettes and creations — is stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no access to your data.

**Are my photos uploaded anywhere?**
Never. All processing happens entirely on your device.

**What data do you collect?**
None. No personal data, no usage analytics, no device identifiers. See our full [Privacy Policy](https://bartlettbutter.github.io/app_public_pages/privacy-policies/huepick/) for details.

**What happens if I delete the app?**
Uninstalling Huepick permanently removes all saved palettes and creations from your device. Since there is no cloud backup or sync, this cannot be undone.

## Troubleshooting

**The camera isn't working.**
Go to your device's Settings → Huepick and confirm Camera access is enabled. If it still doesn't work, force-close the app and reopen it.

**Extracted colors don't look right.**
Color extraction works best with well-lit, clearly focused photos. Try natural lighting, avoid extreme shadows or blown-out highlights, and aim for a variety of tones in the frame.

**A creative tool seems slow.**
All processing runs on your device's hardware. Larger, higher-resolution photos may take a moment — this is expected behavior, not a bug.

**The app keeps crashing.**
Update to the latest version from the App Store and restart your device. If crashes continue, please reach out to us with your device model and iOS version.

Welcome to the Huepick support page. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does color extraction work?

Huepick analyzes your photo on-device to identify the dominant colors. All image processing happens locally on your device, and your photos are never uploaded anywhere.

### Do I need an internet connection?

Color extraction, palette generation, saving, and editing all work offline. The only feature that uses an internet connection is displaying the location name on saved palettes (if your photo contains location data). This is optional and the app works fully without it.

### What are the harmony types?

Huepick offers six harmony types based on classical color theory. Each determines how palette colors relate to each other:

- **Complementary** — Colors opposite on the color wheel for bold contrast
- **Analogous** — Neighboring colors for a harmonious, serene feel
- **Triadic** — Three evenly spaced colors for vibrant balance
- **Split Complementary** — A color plus two adjacent to its complement for nuanced contrast
- **Tetradic** — Two complementary pairs for a rich, diverse palette
- **Monochromatic** — Variations of one hue for elegant cohesion

### What are the use cases?

Use cases tailor your palette for a specific real-world scenario. Each assigns meaningful color roles:

- **Home Decor** — Wall, floor, and accent colors
- **Car Interior** — Panel, seat, and accent colors
- **Fashion** — Top, bottom, and accessory colors
- **Makeup** — Lip, eye, blush, and highlight colors
- **UI & Graphic Design** — Primary, secondary, accent, surface, background, and text colors

### How do I copy a hex code?

Tap any color swatch in a palette to copy its hex code to your clipboard. A checkmark will briefly appear to confirm the copy.

### Can I create a custom palette?

Yes. After extracting colors from a photo, tap "Create Custom Palette" on the Palettes screen. You can pick from your extracted colors, add custom colors using the color picker, or start from a recommended scenario palette and adjust it.

### Where is my data stored?

All saved palettes are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

### How do I delete saved palettes?

Swipe left on a palette in the Gallery to delete it. You'll be asked to confirm before it's removed.

### Can I pin palettes?

Yes. You can pin palettes to keep them at the top of your Gallery for quick access.

### Is Huepick free?

Yes, Huepick is completely free with no in-app purchases and no ads.

### Is my data private?

Yes. Your photos never leave your device and no personal data is collected. See our full [Privacy Policy](https://bartlettbutter.github.io/app_public_pages/privacy-policies/huepick/) for details.

## Troubleshooting

### The camera isn't working

Make sure you've granted Huepick camera access in **Settings > Huepick > Camera**. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

### The app is crashing

Please make sure you're running the latest version of Huepick from the App Store. If the problem continues, try restarting your device.

### Colors don't look accurate

Color extraction works best with clear, well-lit photos. Try taking the photo in better lighting or from a different angle. Very dark or very bright images may produce less distinct colors.

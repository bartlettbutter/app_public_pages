Welcome to the Solcast support page. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

## About Solcast

### What does Solcast do?

Solcast is a personalized weather companion that translates real-time weather data into actionable lifestyle guidance. It provides 16 lifestyle indices, outfit recommendations, and a daily briefing, all tailored to your preferences. It tells you what to wear, what to watch out for, and how to plan your day.

### Who is Solcast for?

Anyone who wants weather presented as practical, personalized advice. It's especially useful for parents, pet owners, allergy sufferers, migraine-sensitive individuals, outdoor exercisers, and anyone who wants to dress appropriately without overthinking it.

### Is Solcast free?

Yes, Solcast is completely free with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads.

### Do I need an internet connection?

An internet connection is needed to fetch weather data. Once loaded, cached forecasts are available offline. All recommendations and indices are computed on your device regardless of connectivity.

## Getting Started

### How do I set up Solcast?

1. **Launch the app** — a Welcome screen appears
2. **Grant location permission** when prompted — this allows Solcast to fetch weather for your current area (you can skip this and add locations manually instead)
3. **View your dashboard** — weather data, outfit recommendations, and your daily briefing load automatically
4. **Personalize in Settings** (gear icon, top right) — configure temperature preference, dressing style, sun sensitivity, and lifestyle factors

No account creation or login is required. All features are immediately accessible.

### How do I personalize my experience?

Open Settings to configure 11 options across three sections:

- **About You** — temperature unit, temperature sensitivity, sun sensitivity, eye sensitivity, allergy sensitivity, migraine sensitivity
- **Your Style** — dressing style, outdoor exercise preference
- **Your Household** — kids, pets, cars

Changes take effect instantly on the dashboard without needing to reload or reconnect.

### How many locations can I save?

You can save up to 10 locations and switch between them using the location picker on the dashboard. You can also search for cities manually without granting location permission.

## Permissions

### Why does Solcast need my location?

Solcast requests **Location (When In Use)** permission to fetch accurate weather forecasts, air quality, and pollen data for your current area. Your coordinates are sent only to weather services (Apple Weather and Open-Meteo) and are never stored on any server. You can also add locations manually without granting location access.

### Does Solcast access my camera, contacts, or other data?

No. Solcast only requests location permission. It does not access your camera, contacts, microphone, photos, health data, or any other sensitive information.

## Privacy & Data

### Is my data private?

Yes. All recommendations are computed on your device. No personal data is collected or transmitted. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no ads. See our full [Privacy Policy](https://bartlettbutter.github.io/app_public_pages/privacy-policies/solcast/) for details.

## External Services

Solcast uses the following services to deliver weather data:

| Service | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Apple Weather** | Primary weather source: temperature, wind, UV, precipitation, sunrise/sunset, and more |
| **Open-Meteo** | Fallback weather source, plus exclusive provider of air quality (AQI, PM2.5, PM10) and pollen forecasts |
| **Apple Maps** | Location search and city name display |

All recommendation logic (16 lifestyle indices, outfit suggestions, daily briefing) runs entirely on your device with no external service or AI involved.

## Troubleshooting

### Weather data isn't loading

Check that your device is connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data. Try pulling down on the dashboard to refresh. If the problem persists, the weather service may be temporarily unavailable. Try again in a few minutes.

### Location isn't being detected

Make sure you've granted Solcast location access in **Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Solcast**. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or toggling location permission off and on.

### The app is crashing

Please make sure you're running the latest version of Solcast from the App Store. If the problem continues, try restarting your device. If crashes persist, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

## Regional Availability

Solcast works worldwide with no regional differences in features or content. Weather data coverage depends on Apple Weather and Open-Meteo, both of which provide global coverage. Air quality and pollen data availability may vary by region. The app will simply hide indices when data is unavailable for your area.

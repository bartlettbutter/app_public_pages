Welcome to the Solcast support page. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does Solcast do?

Solcast provides personalized weather forecasts with lifestyle indices, outfit recommendations, and a daily briefing — all tailored to your preferences.

### Do I need an internet connection?

An internet connection is needed to fetch weather data. Once loaded, cached forecasts are available offline. All recommendations and indices are computed on your device.

### How do I personalize my experience?

Open Settings to configure options like temperature preference, dressing style, sun sensitivity, and lifestyle factors. Changes take effect instantly.

### How many locations can I save?

You can save multiple locations and switch between them using the location picker on the dashboard.

### Is my data private?

Yes. All recommendations are computed on your device. No personal data is collected or transmitted. See our full [Privacy Policy](https://bartlettbutter.github.io/app_public_pages/privacy-policies/solcast/) for details.

### Is Solcast free?

Yes, Solcast is completely free with no in-app purchases and no ads.

## Troubleshooting

### Weather data isn't loading

Check that your device is connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data. Try pulling down on the dashboard to refresh.

### Location isn't being detected

Make sure you've granted Solcast location access in **Settings > Solcast > Location**. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

### The app is crashing

Please make sure you're running the latest version of Solcast from the App Store. If the problem continues, try restarting your device.

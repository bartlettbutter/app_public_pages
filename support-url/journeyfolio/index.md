Welcome to the Journeyfolio support page. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does Journeyfolio cost?

Journeyfolio is a one-time purchase on the App Store. There are no subscriptions and no ads.

### Where is my trip data stored?

All trip information — destinations, dates, activities, flight numbers, hotel details, confirmation numbers, and notes — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.

### Do I need an internet connection?

Your trip data is stored locally and accessible offline. An internet connection is only needed for live weather forecasts, flight status updates, public holiday information, and location search.

### Can I plan a trip with multiple cities?

Yes. You can add multiple destination cities to a single trip. Each city gets its own itinerary section, weather forecast, and date range. Tap a multi-city trip to see all your destinations, then tap a city to view its activities.

### How do I get weather forecasts?

Add a destination city and Journeyfolio will automatically fetch current conditions and a 5-day forecast using [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html). If Apple Weather is unavailable, OpenWeatherMap is used as a fallback.

### How does flight tracking work?

Add a flight number and date to your itinerary. Journeyfolio will fetch live status and schedule information from FlightAware, showing departure/arrival times, airports, airline name, and status badges (On Time, Delayed, Cancelled, etc.) directly on your flight card.

### How do public holidays work?

Journeyfolio automatically displays public holidays for your trip destinations using data from Nager.Date. Holiday dates are highlighted in your itinerary so you can plan around closures and events.

### What types of activities can I add?

Journeyfolio supports seven categories:

- Flights
- Hotels
- Transport (car rentals, trains, buses, ferries, taxis, shuttles)
- Restaurants
- Places & Tickets (attractions, museums, shows, tours, parks, shopping)
- Insurance
- Other

### How do I find hotels and restaurants near my destination?

When adding an activity, use the built-in location search to find nearby places. Results include the name, address, and phone number, which are automatically added to your activity.

### How do I pin a trip?

Swipe right on a trip in your list to pin it. Pinned trips stay at the top for quick access.

### How do I delete a trip?

Swipe left on a trip in your list to delete it. All associated activities and details will be removed.

## Troubleshooting

### Weather forecasts aren't loading

Make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data. If the issue persists, check that the destination city name is spelled correctly. Weather data is provided by [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html).

### Flight status isn't updating

Verify that the flight number and date are entered correctly. Flight data requires an internet connection and is provided by FlightAware — there may be occasional delays in availability. Live status is available for flights within 24 hours of departure.

### Location search isn't returning results

Make sure you have an internet connection. Try a more specific search term, or check that your destination city is set correctly.

### The app is crashing

Make sure you're running the latest version of Journeyfolio from the App Store. If the problem continues, try restarting your device.

### My trip data seems to be missing

Trip data is stored locally and preserved across app updates. Try restarting the app or your device. Note that uninstalling the app will permanently delete all trip data. If your data is still missing, please email us at [bartlettbutter@gmail.com](mailto:bartlettbutter@gmail.com).

## Getting Started

### How much does Journeyfolio cost?

Journeyfolio is a one-time purchase on the App Store. No subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases. Buy it once and it's yours.

### Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no sign-up, no login, and no account to manage. Open the app and start planning immediately.

### What iOS version do I need?

Journeyfolio requires iOS 18.0 or later on iPhone.

## Your Data

### Where is my trip data stored?

Everything — destinations, dates, activities, flight numbers, hotel details, confirmation numbers, and notes — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. There is no cloud sync or backup.

### Is my data private?

Yes. Journeyfolio collects no personal data, has no analytics, and runs no advertising. Your trip information stays on your device. See our full [Privacy Policy](https://bartlettbutter.github.io/app_public_pages/privacy-policies/journeyfolio/) for details.

### What happens if I delete the app?

Uninstalling Journeyfolio permanently removes all stored trip data. Since there is no cloud backup, this cannot be undone. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your data.

## Trip Planning

### Can I plan a trip with multiple cities?

Yes. Add multiple destination cities to a single trip. Each city gets its own itinerary section, weather forecast, date range, and public holidays. Tap a multi-city trip to see all destinations, then tap a city to view its activities.

### What types of activities can I add?

Seven categories cover everything you need:

- **Flights** — Flight number, seat, confirmation number, and live status
- **Hotels** — Address, phone, check-in/check-out dates, and notes
- **Transport** — Car rentals, trains, buses, ferries, rideshares, subways, shuttles
- **Restaurants** — Address and phone number
- **Places & Tickets** — Attractions, museums, events, tours, parks, shopping
- **Insurance** — Travel insurance details
- **Other** — Anything else

### Can activities span multiple days?

Yes. Toggle "Multi-day" when adding an activity (like a hotel stay or car rental) to set start and end dates. The activity appears on each day it spans.

### How do I find hotels and restaurants near my destination?

Use the built-in location search when adding a hotel, restaurant, transport, or places activity. Type a name or keyword, tap a result to auto-fill details, and see a map preview of the location.

### How do I pin or delete a trip?

- **Pin:** Swipe right on a trip to pin it to the top of your list. Swipe right again to unpin.
- **Delete:** Swipe left on a trip or activity. A confirmation prompt appears before permanent deletion.

## Weather & Flights

### How do weather forecasts work?

Add a destination city and Journeyfolio automatically fetches current conditions and a 5-day forecast using [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html). Toggle between °C and °F on the weather card. If Apple Weather is temporarily unavailable, OpenWeatherMap is used as a fallback.

### How does flight tracking work?

Add a flight activity, enter your flight number and departure date, then tap "Verify Flight." Journeyfolio auto-fills the route, airline, and departure/arrival times (displayed in each airport's local timezone). For flights within 24 hours of departure, live status badges — On Time, Delayed, Cancelled, Landed, In Air — appear on your flight card.

Schedule verification covers flights up to 12 months in the future and 3 months in the past. For dates outside this range, enter details manually.

### How do public holidays work?

Journeyfolio automatically displays public holidays for your destination's country. Holiday dates are highlighted in the date strip with a soft yellow background. Tap a holiday date to see the holiday name.

### Do I need an internet connection?

Your trip data is always accessible offline. An internet connection is only needed for:
- Live weather forecasts
- Flight verification and status updates
- Public holiday information
- Location search

## Troubleshooting

### Weather forecasts aren't loading

Check that your device has an internet connection and that the destination city name is correct. Weather data is provided by [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html) with OpenWeatherMap as a fallback.

### Flight verification says "No flight found"

- Double-check the flight number format (e.g., "JL001" or "AA100").
- Confirm the departure date is correct.
- Verification covers 3 months past to 12 months future. For dates outside this range, use manual entry.
- Some regional or charter flights may not be in the database.

### Flight status isn't showing

Live status badges only appear for flights within approximately 24 hours of departure. Verified schedule information (route, times, airline) is still displayed for flights further out.

### Location search isn't returning results

Ensure you have an internet connection. Try a more specific term (e.g., "Hilton Tokyo" instead of "hotel"). Location search works best with specific place names.

### Public holidays aren't showing

Holidays require a valid country for your destination. If the destination couldn't be resolved to a country, try a more specific city name (e.g., "Paris, France").

### The app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly

1. Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store.
2. Restart the app.
3. Restart your device.
4. If the issue persists, email us with a description of what happened.

### My trip data seems to be missing

Trip data is preserved across app updates. Try restarting the app or your device. Note that uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data (no cloud backup). If data is still missing after a restart, please contact us.

Welcome to the Journeyfolio support page. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does Journeyfolio cost?

Journeyfolio is a one-time purchase on the App Store. There are no subscriptions and no ads.

### Where is my trip data stored?

All trip information, including destinations, dates, activities, flight numbers, hotel details, confirmation numbers, and notes, is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. There is no cloud sync or backup.

### Do I need an internet connection?

Your trip data is stored locally and accessible offline. An internet connection is only needed for:
- Live weather forecasts
- Flight schedule verification and status updates
- Public holiday information
- Location search (finding hotels, restaurants, and points of interest)

### Can I plan a trip with multiple cities?

Yes. You can add multiple destination cities to a single trip. Each city gets its own itinerary section, weather forecast, date range, and public holidays. Tap a multi-city trip to see all your destinations, then tap a city to view its activities.

### How do I get weather forecasts?

Add a destination city and Journeyfolio will automatically fetch current conditions and a 5-day forecast using [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html). You can toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit directly on the weather card. If Apple Weather is temporarily unavailable, OpenWeatherMap is used as a fallback.

### How does flight tracking work?

Add a flight activity and enter your flight number and departure date. Tap "Verify Flight" to look up the schedule. Journeyfolio will auto-fill the route, airline, and departure/arrival times (displayed in each airport's local timezone). For flights within 24 hours, live status badges (On Time, Delayed, Cancelled, Landed, In Air) appear on your flight card.

Schedule verification is available for flights up to 12 months in the future and 3 months in the past. For dates outside this range, you can enter flight details manually.

### How do public holidays work?

Journeyfolio automatically displays public holidays for your trip destination's country. Holiday dates are highlighted in the date strip with a soft yellow background. Tap a holiday date to see the holiday name displayed below the date strip.

### What types of activities can I add?

Journeyfolio supports seven categories:

- **Flights** — With flight number, seat, confirmation number, and live status
- **Hotels** — With address, phone, check-in/check-out dates, and notes
- **Transport** — Car rentals, trains, buses, ferries, taxis/rideshares, subways, shuttles
- **Restaurants** — With address and phone number
- **Places & Tickets** — Attractions, museums, shows/events, tours, parks/nature, shopping
- **Insurance** — Travel insurance details
- **Other** — Anything else

Hotels, transport, restaurants, and places support built-in location search to find and attach a place with its address and phone number.

### Can activities span multiple days?

Yes. When adding an activity (such as a hotel stay or car rental), toggle "Multi-day" to set a start and end date. The activity will appear on each day it spans when you filter by date.

### How do I find hotels and restaurants near my destination?

When adding a hotel, restaurant, transport, or places activity, use the built-in location search. Type a name or keyword and results will appear with name, address, and phone number. Tap a result to auto-fill the details and see a map preview of the location.

### How do I pin a trip?

Swipe right on a trip in your list to pin it. Pinned trips stay at the top in a dedicated section for quick access. Swipe right again to unpin.

### How do I delete a trip or activity?

Swipe left on a trip or activity to delete it. A confirmation prompt will appear before deletion. All associated data will be permanently removed.

### What does the destination input support?

You can type a city name (e.g., "Tokyo"), a country name (e.g., "France", which resolves to Paris), or even a misspelled name. Journeyfolio uses smart matching to resolve your input to the correct destination.

### Is my data private?

Yes. All trip data stays on your device. No personal data is collected or transmitted. See our full [Privacy Policy](https://bartlettbutter.github.io/app_public_pages/privacy-policies/journeyfolio/) for details.

## Troubleshooting

### Weather forecasts aren't loading

Make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data. If the issue persists, check that the destination city name is spelled correctly. Weather data is provided by [Apple Weather](https://weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html) with OpenWeatherMap as a fallback.

### Flight verification says "No flight found"

- Verify that the flight number is entered correctly (e.g., "JL001" or "AA100").
- Check that the departure date is correct.
- Schedule verification is available for flights within 3 months in the past to 12 months in the future. For dates outside this range, use manual entry.
- Some regional or charter flights may not be available in the database.

### Flight status isn't showing

Live flight status is only available for flights within approximately 24 hours of departure. For flights further out, the verified schedule information (route, times, airline) will still be displayed.

### Location search isn't returning results

Make sure you have an internet connection. Try a more specific search term (e.g., "Hilton Tokyo" instead of "hotel"). Location search works best with specific place names or addresses.

### Public holidays aren't showing

Holidays require a valid country code for your destination. If you entered a destination that couldn't be resolved to a specific country, holidays may not appear. Try editing the destination to a more specific city name (e.g., "Paris, France" instead of just "Paris").

### The app is crashing

Make sure you're running the latest version of Journeyfolio from the App Store. If the problem continues, try restarting your device.

### My trip data seems to be missing

Trip data is stored locally and preserved across app updates. Try restarting the app or your device. Note that uninstalling the app will permanently delete all trip data since there is no cloud backup. If your data is still missing after a restart, please email us at [bartlettbutter@gmail.com](mailto:bartlettbutter@gmail.com).

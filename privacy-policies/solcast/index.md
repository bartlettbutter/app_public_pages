**Effective Date:** May 16, 2026

Solcast ("the App") is a free iOS application that provides personalized weather forecasts, lifestyle indices, and outfit recommendations. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from you. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

## Location Data

The App uses your device's location to retrieve weather forecasts. Location coordinates are sent only to weather services (see Third-Party Services below) to fetch forecast data. Your location is never stored on any server we control.

Saved locations are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted externally except as coordinates in weather API requests.

## User Preferences

Your personalized settings (such as temperature preference and dressing style) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

## On-Device Processing

All lifestyle index calculations, outfit recommendations, and daily briefings are computed entirely on your device. No personal data or preferences are sent to any server for processing.

## Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following third-party services solely to fetch weather and environmental data:

- **Apple WeatherKit** — provides weather forecasts. Requests include only geographic coordinates and are subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **Open-Meteo** (https://open-meteo.com) — provides weather forecasts, air quality data, and pollen forecasts. Requests include only geographic coordinates.
- **Apple MapKit** — provides location search and reverse geocoding, subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

No personal data, device identifiers, or user preferences are sent to these services — only geographic coordinates needed to retrieve forecast data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

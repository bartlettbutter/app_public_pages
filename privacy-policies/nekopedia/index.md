# Nekopedia — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 22, 2026

Nekopedia ("the App") is a free iOS application that identifies cat breeds from photos. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from you. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

## Photos

Photos you take or select are processed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning (CoreML). Your photos are never uploaded to any server.

## Scan History

Breed scan results you save to Favorites are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following third-party APIs solely to fetch content for display:

- **The Cat API** (https://thecatapi.com) — provides breed information, images, and quiz content.
- **catfact.ninja** (https://catfact.ninja) — provides cat facts for the trivia feature.

These requests fetch content only. No personal data or device identifiers are sent to these services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
